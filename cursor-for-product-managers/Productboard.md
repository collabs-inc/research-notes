# Productboard

Closest incumbent to the [[The Full Loop Gap|full loop]] on the discovery side. $262M raised, $1.72B valuation.

## AI Capabilities (Spark AI Agent)

**Spark** launched in public beta January 2026 at $15/maker/month (annual) or $19/month (monthly). 250 credits/maker/month included.

Current capabilities:
- **Multi-channel feedback ingestion** from Intercom, Salesforce, Zendesk, surveys
- **Auto-categorization and theme detection** across all feedback
- **Auto-linking** — AI connects customer insights to relevant features, updating evidence behind prioritization
- **AI gap analysis** — understands product strategy, customer segments, and roadmap history to identify gaps
- **AI-generated feature specs/PRDs** — requires at least 3 linked customer insights (ensures grounding in real data)
- **Spark Jobs** — multi-day automated workflows
- **Prompt Library** — 150+ pre-built AI workflow prompts for PM tasks

## Where It Falls Short

The loop stops at "here's what to build and why." Productboard has no delivery/execution layer — it connects to Jira but doesn't generate executable specs that flow into coding agents. Also requires well-maintained data: if notes are vague or poorly tagged, AI output degrades.

## User Reception

G2: 4.3/5 (253 reviews). Gartner: 4.4/5. Praised for feedback centralization and insight management. Criticized for steep learning curve, clunky UX ("very easy to lose a feature"), limited reporting, high operational overhead with large datasets, and pricing. Requires a dedicated PM to maintain.

Vendor claims 80% boost in feedback processing speed.
