# Gumroad

The creator-payments platform run by [[Sahil Lavingia]] as a radically lean, AI-first, contractor-only company — a near-literal test of the [[The One-Person Company|one-person company]].

- **Structure:** No meetings, no deadlines, no full-time employees. ~25 contributors, all contractors, fully async (GitHub, Notion, occasional Slack) with a 24-hour response norm. People pull tasks from a shared queue. Pay standardized globally ($50/hr support to $250/hr Head of Product); no benefits. Single metric: maximize creator earnings.
- **Output:** Shipped Gumroad Memberships "without a single meeting or video call."
- **AI-first (2025):** Lavingia reports AI agents writing a large, growing share of commits (reported ~41%, targeting ~80%); ~$10M+ ARR (third-party trackers cite higher). Treat exact percentages as secondary.

## Why It Matters

Gumroad is the org-design end-state of [[Small Teams, Big Output]]: a profitable software company with essentially one full-time person plus agents and contractors — "zero management debt." It's the clearest working evidence behind Sam Altman's [[The One-Person Company|one-person company]] bet.
