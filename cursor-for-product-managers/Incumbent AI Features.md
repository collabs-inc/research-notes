# Incumbent AI Features

Comparative analysis of what major PM tool incumbents are doing with AI as of May 2026. Focused on capabilities relevant to product discovery (figuring out *what* to build), not just project management.

See [[The Full Loop Gap]] for why none of these deliver the complete vision.

| Full loop shipped today? | **Closest** (minus execution) | No (pieces in early access) | No (execution tool) | No (DIY platform) | No (assistant, not agent) |

## Key Takeaway

"AI" in most tools means writing assistance. Strip away the marketing and most features are: summarize this text, draft this document, auto-assign this ticket. Genuine intelligence (pattern detection across thousands of signals, evidence-based prioritization, gap analysis) exists only in [[Productboard]] Spark and is coming in [[Atlassian Jira|Atlassian's]] Product Collection.

## Height — Discontinued

Height shut down September 24, 2025 after 3.5 years. Notable as an AI-native project management pioneer (autonomous bug triage, backlog pruning, NLP-driven task management) but couldn't survive competitively. The niche it pioneered was absorbed by Linear and others.
