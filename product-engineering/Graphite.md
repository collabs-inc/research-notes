# Graphite

An AI-powered code-review and stacked-PR platform — serving the *ship* stage of [[The Product Engineering Stack]], the part of the loop that becomes the bottleneck as AI writes more of the code.

- **Funding:** $52M Series B led by Accel (March 2025), with Anthropic's Anthology Fund, Menlo Ventures, Shopify Ventures, Figma Ventures, and a16z. Often described as "Anthropic-backed."

## Why It Matters

Graphite is the clearest entrant in the emerging **"AI maintainer"** category named by Bessemer (see [[Market Landscape]]): when agents generate most code, *review, merge, and maintenance* are where human-and-AI attention concentrates. For [[The Product Engineer|product engineers]] shipping fast, the review/merge layer is the under-tooled bottleneck — and a contested front in [[Competitive Dynamics]].
