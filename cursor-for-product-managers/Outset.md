# Outset

AI-powered user interviews at scale. Best-funded YC company in the adjacent research automation space.

- **Batch:** YC S23
- **Founders:** Aaron Cannon (CEO, ex-Tesla/Pebble head of product), Michael Hess
- **Funding:** $47M total — $17M (June 2025) + $30M led by M12/Microsoft (December 2025)
- **Team:** 30 employees, San Francisco
- **Status:** Active, growing rapidly

## What They Build

LLMs conduct qualitative interviews at scale (video, voice, text), in any language, then synthesize results. "High quality data 100x faster."

## Relevance to "Cursor for PMs"

Automates the data *collection* step of the [[The Full Loop Gap|full loop]] but not the synthesis-to-spec step. A "Cursor for PMs" tool would likely need to ingest the kind of interview data Outset produces.
