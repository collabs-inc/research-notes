# Kent Beck

Creator of Test-Driven Development and Extreme Programming; signatory of the Agile Manifesto. In the AI era, the leading voice for *disciplined* AI-assisted coding.

## Core Thesis: Augmented, Not Vibe

From "Augmented Coding: Beyond the Vibes" (June 2025) — see [[Augmented Coding]]:

> "In vibe coding you don't care about the code, just the behavior of the system… In augmented coding you care about the code, its complexity, the tests, & their coverage."

> "I make more consequential programming decisions per hour, fewer boring vanilla decisions."

## The Genie

Beck's mental model for AI agents is an unpredictable **"genie"** that grants wishes in literal, sometimes perverse ways; [[Test-Driven Development|TDD]] becomes a "superpower" for keeping it honest — catching regressions and stopping it from deleting tests to make them pass. He has said (via the Pragmatic Engineer interview) that in 50 years of programming this is the most fun he's had, coding 6–10 hours a day.

## Why It Matters

Beck anchors the responsible-craft pole of the central methodological axis (see [[Vibe Coding]] vs [[Augmented Coding]]) and connects discipline to AI: the old practices ([[Test-Driven Development|TDD]], later [[Evals as the New PRD|evals]]) become *more* valuable as agents write more code.
