# shadcn

Creator of **shadcn/ui**, the copy-paste component model that became React's de facto component layer; a [[Design Engineer]] at [[Vercel]] (announced Aug 2023), working on the AI team.

## shadcn/ui

> "Not a component library — it is how you build your component library."

Components are *copied into your codebase as source you own* (built on Radix UI primitives + Tailwind CSS), rather than installed as an npm dependency. The model — own the code, change it freely — reframed how the industry thinks about design systems (see [[Design Systems and Velocity]]) and now drives [[v0]]'s output.

His joining tweet (Aug 2023): *"I'm joining @vercel as a Design Engineer to continue building customizable UI components for the open source community."* [[Guillermo Rauch]]: *"His work has changed the way we think about building and distributing UI."*

> shadcn operates under a pseudonym; his legal name is not reliably public — do not assert one.

## Why It Matters

shadcn is the clearest proof that *distribution model + craft* can beat traditional packaged libraries — the "you own the code" idea is a velocity-and-control unlock central to [[Design Systems and Velocity]] and the [[Craft as a Moat|craft]] movement.
