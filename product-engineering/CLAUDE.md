# Research Vault: Product Engineering

This is an Obsidian-style wiki of 90+ interlinked markdown notes mapping **product engineering** — the convergence of product and engineering into a single role, the *product engineer*, and the practices, people, companies, and tools driving it. It covers three angles: the **role/archetype**, the **practice/methodology**, and the **tools/market**. Current as of June 2026.

The core thesis: as AI collapses the cost of *building* software, the scarce and defensible work becomes *deciding what to build and whether it's good* — judgment and taste. This is the human side of Archetype's bet, complementing the sibling vaults on agentic dev environments (tools for engineers) and "Cursor for product managers" (tools for PMs).

## Structure

- **Concept notes** (e.g., `The Product Engineer.md`, `Taste as the Bottleneck.md`, `Everyone Is a Builder.md`) explain one idea each.
- **Role notes** (e.g., `Design Engineer.md`, `Forward-Deployed Engineer.md`) define one role and its boundary with the product engineer.
- **Practice notes** (e.g., `Vibe Coding.md`, `Spec-Driven Development.md`, `Shape Up.md`) cover one method each.
- **Company notes** (e.g., `PostHog.md`, `Linear.md`, `Shopify.md`) profile how one org structures product + engineering.
- **Person notes** (e.g., `Gergely Orosz.md`, `Guillermo Rauch.md`) profile one thinker's thesis.
- **Index/hub notes** (`The Role and Its Variants.md`, `Company Case Studies.md`, `Thinkers and Voices.md`, `Practices and Methods.md`) are slim hubs linking the atomic notes.
- **Market notes** (`Market Landscape.md`, `Competitive Dynamics.md`, `Labor Market.md`, `The Product Engineering Stack.md`) cover the business landscape.

## A Note on Sourcing

This is a fast-moving 2024–2026 topic. Well-verified facts are stated plainly; figures that are secondary, company-reported, or contested are hedged inline ("reportedly," "company-stated," "treat as approximate"). Private-company ARR/headcount numbers vary across trackers and should be read as best-available estimates.
