# Auxos

"Digital twins for market research."

- **Batch:** YC P26 (Spring 2026) — one of the first companies from the batch that included the [[YC RFS Spring 2026|"Cursor for PMs" RFS]]
- **Founders:** Ashton Daniel (CEO, ex-BCG agentic AI consultant, Columbia CS), Kerry Lu (CTO, ex-Amazon Ads, ex-Vorticity/YC S19, Duke CS/Math), Jerry Wu (CPO, ex-Meta/Instagram, UPenn CS). All three met fencing for Team USA; 15-year friendship.
- **Funding:** YC standard deal
- **Team:** 3 people, San Francisco
- **Status:** Very early stage

## What They Build

AI customer twins let teams test hypotheses (ad creative, pricing, landing pages, product decisions) in minutes vs. weeks. Synthetic market research for product validation.

## Relevance to "Cursor for PMs"

Upstream from the RFS vision — tests "would customers want this?" without real panels. Part of the synthetic research cluster alongside [[Artificial Societies]].
