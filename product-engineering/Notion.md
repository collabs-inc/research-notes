# Notion

The productivity-software company that reached unicorn scale with a few dozen people by hiring [[The Generalist|generalists]] and prioritizing "systems over headcount."

- **Model:** Compact, high-leverage teams owning large product areas; "stay small to move fast." Hit unicorn status with a few dozen employees.
- **Hiring:** Generalists — "a designer who can code, an engineer with strong product sense, PMs not afraid to roll up their sleeves." Built in the founders' image: Ivan Zhao is a designer who codes (with Simon Last). "Talent compounds" — hire "exceptional," not "good enough."

## Why It Matters

Notion is a textbook [[Small Teams, Big Output|small-team]] / [[The Generalist|generalist]] org: the [[The Product Engineer|product-engineer]] disposition (build + product sense + design) as a *hiring filter*, not a single title. It is also where [[Brian Lovin]] and [[Geoffrey Litt]] now build AI products — a hub for [[Design Engineer|design-engineering]] and [[Malleable Software|malleable-software]] thinking.
