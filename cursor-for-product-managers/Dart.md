# Dart

"Cursor for Project Management." Chat-based, AI-native project management.

- **Batch:** YC W22
- **Founders:** Milad Malek (Stanford physics, ex-Plug and Play Ventures), Zack Swafford (Stanford, ex-Plenty first engineer)
- **Traction:** Thousands of teams. Claims 7 hrs/week saved. 10 employees.
- **Location:** San Francisco
- **Status:** Active

## What They Build

Chat as the primary UI, AI agents as first-class collaborators. Users create projects, tasks, subtasks, timelines, specs, and reports through a conversational interface. Customizable AI agents for marketing, design, sales, coding roles.

From their YC launch page: "Most PM tools add AI features. Dart is fundamentally designed around AI."

## Relevance to "Cursor for PMs"

Positions explicitly as "Cursor for *Project* Management" — emphasis on execution/tracking. Focuses on HOW to manage work, not WHAT to build. Adjacent to but distinct from the product discovery vision in [[YC RFS Spring 2026]].
