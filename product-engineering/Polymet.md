# Polymet

An "AI Product Designer" (YC S24) — describe or upload an image and get designed UI plus production-ready frontend code, round-tripping with Figma.

- **Funding:** $2M pre-seed (Sept 2024), investors including Y Combinator, e2vc, Factorial Capital, and Pioneer Fund. Founder/CEO: Yusuf Hilmi Kunt.

## Why It Matters

Polymet is a net-new YC-backed entrant branding itself a "Product Designer" *for non-designers* — a direct expression of the [[Everyone Is a Builder]] / generalist-empowerment thesis, and part of the prototype-from-intent layer in [[The Product Engineering Stack]]. Its positioning (the tool *is* the product designer) is a small instance of the role-collapse this vault tracks.
