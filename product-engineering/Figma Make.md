# Figma Make

[[Figma]]'s "AI product builder" — turn a text prompt into a clickable, interactive prototype/app inside Figma, "no PRD or coding required." Announced at Config 2025 (May 2025), GA July 2025; output in React + Tailwind, initially powered by Claude.

## Why It Matters

Figma Make is the incumbent design platform moving *into* the prototype-and-build stage of [[The Product Engineering Stack]] — a key data point in [[Competitive Dynamics]], where the prototype layer is being squeezed from design (Figma) on one side and deploy ([[Vercel]]/[[v0]]) on the other. Early reviews (e.g. "Great Ideas, Nowhere to Go," 2025) flag a gap between prototype and shippable handoff — the same "prototype vs. product" boundary [[Build to Learn vs Build to Earn]] warns about.
