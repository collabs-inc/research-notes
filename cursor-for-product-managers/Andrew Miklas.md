# Andrew Miklas

Author of the [[YC RFS Spring 2026|"Cursor for Product Managers" RFS category]].

- Co-founded **PagerDuty** (YC S10, NYSE: PD) as founding CTO
- Early-stage investor at **s28 Capital**, backing Clerk, CaptivateIQ, and Teleport
- Promoted to **YC General Partner** alongside Jon Xu on May 21, 2025 (both originally went through YC in 2010)

His core thesis: AI coding tools have solved how to build software, but the upstream problem — figuring out *what* to build — remains "almost entirely unsolved." Traditional PM artifacts (PRDs, Figma mocks, Jira tickets) were designed for human engineers and break down as AI agents take the first pass at implementation.
