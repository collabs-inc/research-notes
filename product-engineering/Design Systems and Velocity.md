# Design Systems and Velocity

Design systems are the lever that converts [[Craft|craft]] into repeatable speed — and the mechanism by which one person's [[Taste as the Bottleneck|taste]] scales to a whole org (and now to AI agents).

## How They Create Velocity

- **Tokens remove the parity tax:** design tokens as CSS variables mean "designers say 'primary-500,' engineers ship the same color in minutes — no drift," eliminating the classic Figma↔code mismatch.
- **Owned-source components:** [[shadcn]]/ui reframed the model — components are *copied into your codebase as source you own* (built on Radix + Tailwind), not installed as a dependency. "Not a component library — it's how you build your component library." The velocity win comes from control + composability.
- **No handoff:** [[Linear]]'s connected teams share quality ownership with no design→dev handoff — itself a velocity-and-craft mechanism.

## The AI Connection

Feeding a design system into AI generation keeps brand fidelity while accelerating output — [[Vercel]] calls this "AI-powered prototyping with design systems," and tools like [[v0]], [[Figma Make]], and [[Magic Patterns]] generate against a system's tokens. The design system is where *taste scales*: it encodes craft so an org — or an agent — inherits it.
