# Product Engineering MOC

Map of content for research on **product engineering** — the convergence of product and engineering into a single role, the [[The Product Engineer|product engineer]], and the practices, people, companies, and tools driving it. The throughline: as AI collapses the cost of *building*, the scarce, defensible work becomes *deciding what to build and whether it's good* — see [[Taste as the Bottleneck]]. Current as of June 2026.

This vault covers all three angles — the **role**, the **practice**, and the **market** — and complements the sibling vaults on [[agentic-dev-envs/Agentic Development Environments MOC|agentic dev environments]] (tools for engineers) and [[cursor-for-product-managers/Cursor for Product Managers MOC|Cursor for product managers]] (tools for PMs).
