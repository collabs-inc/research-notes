# Kraftful

"Copilot for product teams." AI analysis of user feedback across channels. Acquired by Amplitude.

- **Batch:** YC S19
- **Founders:** Yana Welinder (ex-IFTTT head of product)
- **Funding:** ~$6M from YC, Google, Samsung
- **Traction:** 50,000+ teams pre-acquisition. Named Fast Company Most Innovative Company 2024.
- **Team:** 6 people, San Francisco
- **Status:** Acquired by Amplitude (July 2025). Platform shut down August 2025. Now "Amplitude AI Feedback."

## What They Built

AI analysis of user feedback across channels (interviews, surveys, app reviews, support tickets, sales calls). Hallucination detection. AI-generated conversational surveys.
