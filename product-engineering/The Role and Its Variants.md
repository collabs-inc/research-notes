# The Role and Its Variants

A hub for the [[The Product Engineer|product engineer]] and the cluster of adjacent titles AI is reshaping. The unifying ethos across all of them is the **builder who collapses roles and ships end-to-end** — what differs is the surface they own.

## The Core

- [[The Product Engineer]] — owns the product outcome end-to-end; hybrid of SWE + PM
- [[The Product-Minded Software Engineer]] — the mindset (Orosz, 2019) the title formalized

## The Distinctions (what product engineer is *not*)

| Role | Owns | Key fault line vs. product engineer |
|---|---|---|
| [[Product Engineer vs Software Engineer\|Software engineer]] | The code's reliability | build great products vs. build reliable software |
| [[Product Engineer vs Product Manager\|Product manager]] | Specs, alignment | ships code vs. orchestrates |
| [[Design Engineer]] | The craft/UI surface | whole product vs. interface craft |
| [[Forward-Deployed Engineer]] | One customer | reusable (many users) vs. bespoke (one customer) |
| [[Founding Engineer]] | The earliest build | durable orientation vs. stage-bound title |
| [[Growth Engineer]] | Metric movement | ship to build vs. ship to learn |
| [[Platform Engineer]] | Internal infra | external/customer-facing vs. internal-facing |
| [[AI Engineer]] | Shipping AI products | product judgment vs. ML/model fluency |
