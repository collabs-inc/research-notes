# Replit

The browser IDE turned AI app-builder, led by [[Amjad Masad]] — included here for its thesis ("everyone is a software creator") and its lean, agent-driven org.

- **Thesis:** Not a billion software *engineers* but a billion software *creators* — non-technical people who describe what they want and let agents build it. "The future of software is agents all the way down." See [[Everyone Is a Builder]].
- **Org:** Masad cut headcount sharply during a restructuring (reported ~120 → ~60 "skeleton crew") before growing again; notably shifted to sales being more than half the company by late 2025. Product centers on the autonomous **Agent**.
- **Scale (secondary, conflicting):** ARR reported climbing from ~$2.7M (2024) to ~$70–150M+ (2025); ~$400M raised at a ~$9B valuation (2026). Treat figures as press-reported.

## Why It Matters

Replit is the consumer-facing edge of [[Everyone Is a Builder]] — pushing the "builder" frontier to people who don't code at all. (The product is profiled in the sibling vault [[agentic-dev-envs/Replit]] and [[agentic-dev-envs/Replit Agent]].)
