# Research Vault: Cursor for Product Managers

This is an Obsidian-style wiki of interlinked markdown notes mapping the "Cursor for Product Managers" market opportunity — the AI tools that help product teams figure out *what* to build, not just how to build it. Covers the YC RFS that named the category, funded startups, incumbent responses, non-YC competitors, and practitioner sentiment as of late May 2026.

The category was named by [[Andrew Miklas]] in YC's [[YC RFS Spring 2026|Spring 2026 Request for Startups]].

## Where to Start

- **[[Cursor for Product Managers MOC]]** is the map of content. Start here for any question.
- **[[YC RFS Spring 2026]]** for the original thesis and what YC asked for.
- **[[The Full Loop Gap]]** for the core concept — why nobody has built this yet.
- **[[Market Landscape]]** for sizing, structure, and consolidation patterns.
- Notes use `[[wiki-links]]` — follow them to navigate.

## Structure

- **Concept notes** (e.g., `The Full Loop Gap.md`, `Practitioner Sentiment.md`) explain one idea or theme
- **Entity notes** (e.g., `Productboard.md`, `Inari.md`, `ChatPRD.md`) cover one company each
- **Index notes** (e.g., `YC-Funded Startups.md`, `Incumbent AI Features.md`) are hubs linking to entity notes
- **Market notes** (`Market Landscape.md`, `Competitive Dynamics.md`) cover the business landscape
