# Continuous Discovery

Weekly (minimum) customer touchpoints by the team building the product, running small research activities in pursuit of a desired outcome. The methodology of [[Teresa Torres]], from *Continuous Discovery Habits* (2021).

## Definition

> "at a minimum, weekly touchpoints with customers, by the team building the product, where they conduct small research activities, in pursuit of a desired outcome."

The companion artifact is the **Opportunity Solution Tree** (Torres, 2016): outcome at the top → opportunities → solutions → assumption tests.

## The AI Twist

The notable 2025 development: Torres herself began [[Vibe Coding|vibe coding]] in March 2025 with no engineering background, reporting she wrote more software in six months than in her lifetime, and shipped a production AI product (an interview-coaching tool). She frames vibe coding as *"particularly powerful for assumption testing"* — building interactive prototypes to validate in hours.

Her caution: whether PMs *should* build depends on "your org setup, not the tools," and the risk is that they "jump to the solution space all too soon." Tagline: *"just because you can doesn't mean you should."* This is the discovery-side mirror of the [[The Product Engineer|product engineer]] who can now build what they discover.
