# Artificial Societies

Networks of AI personas simulating stakeholder opinions and audience reactions.

- **Batch:** YC W25
- **Founders:** James He (CEO, Cambridge computational social scientist, ex-Yonder), Patrick Sharpe (CPO, MSc behavioral economics, ex-Swiss Re/iptiQ)
- **Funding:** $5.35M seed (Aug 2025)
- **Traction:** 95% accuracy claim in simulating human opinions. 18M+ responses. $100M+ in decisions influenced. Fortune 100 enterprise clients.
- **Team:** 6 employees, London, UK
- **Status:** Active

## What They Build

Platform "Radiant" creates networks of 300-5,000+ AI personas that simulate how opinions spread. Uses real-world social behavior data to model audience reactions.

## Relevance to "Cursor for PMs"

Instead of collecting real customer feedback, simulate it. Could replace or augment the interview step in the [[The Full Loop Gap|full loop]] pipeline — test product hypotheses against synthetic customer panels before committing resources.
