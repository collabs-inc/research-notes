# Figma

The design platform whose org has a famously "permeable membrane" between design, engineering, and product — and whose CEO has become a leading voice on craft as the AI-era moat.

- **Model:** Early teams were ~1 designer, 1 PM, and a few engineers, with a "permeable membrane" letting people flow between teams. Engineers join design crits, observe user research, and engage customers directly in private-beta Slack channels. The open, transparent collaboration mirrors the product itself.
- **Craft thesis:** Dylan Field argues AI makes "design, craft, and quality the new moat" — as building commoditizes, the felt quality of a product is what differentiates (see [[Craft as a Moat]], [[Taste as the Bottleneck]]).

## In the Stack

Figma is also a *tooling* player in the [[The Product Engineering Stack|product-engineering stack]]: **Dev Mode** (design→code handoff, plus a Dev Mode MCP server for agents) and **[[Figma Make]]** (prompt-to-app, 2025) move it from design into prototype and build.

## Why It Matters

Figma embodies the [[Design Engineer|design-engineering]] ethos at the org level — engineers and designers close together, customer-immersed — and its leadership is articulating the [[Craft as a Moat|craft-as-moat]] argument that underpins much of this vault.
