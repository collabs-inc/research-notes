# Paco Coursey

A [[Design Engineer]] on [[Linear]]'s web team (previously at [[Vercel]]); creator of **cmdk**, the ubiquitous command-menu React component.

## Notable Work

- **⌘K / cmdk** — a fast, composable command-menu component used across the industry.
- At [[Vercel]] (intern → full-time) he worked on the design system, dashboard, data-fetching standards, marketing site, and a rebrand.
- Design philosophy: *"what we call a delightful user experience is just delivering a faster path to user goals"* — paired with obsessive detail work on motion, typography, and keyboard accessibility.

## Why It Matters

Coursey exemplifies the open-source-primitive route to influence: a single well-crafted component (cmdk) seeds interaction patterns across thousands of apps. He's part of the [[Vercel]]→[[Linear]] [[Design Engineer|design-engineer]] pipeline that carries the [[Craft as a Moat|craft]] standard.
