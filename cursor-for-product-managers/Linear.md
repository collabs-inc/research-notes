# Linear

Execution-focused PM tool. Explicitly stays in its lane — tracking and shipping work, not deciding what to build.

## AI Capabilities

- **Triage Intelligence** (all plans including Free) — learns from historical patterns to auto-suggest assignees, teams, labels, projects. Accuracy improves with 3+ months of history.
- **Product Intelligence** — broader AI layer for auto-categorization and team routing. New, under a "Product Intelligence" umbrella.
- **Linear Agent** (Business plan, $16/user/mo) — works on issues end-to-end, connects to external tools via MCP.
- **Agent-powered integrations** — Zendesk, Intercom, Gong connectors turn customer conversations into triaged Linear issues.
- **Semantic search**, duplicate detection, AI-generated pulse summaries.
- **Code Intelligence** (coming, Business+) — extends agent understanding to codebases for diagnosing and writing specs.

## Pricing

Free (250 issues, 2 teams), Basic ($10/user/mo), Business ($16/user/mo), Enterprise (custom, ~$250-350/user/year). AI features on Free/Basic are limited; Agent and Code Intelligence require Business+.

## Relevance to "Cursor for PMs"

Linear's AI is firmly "manage and route work," not "figure out what to build." Product Intelligence automates triage, not synthesis. The Zendesk/Intercom/Gong integrations get partway to customer signals but there's no feedback aggregation, theme detection, or prioritization framework. Linear is an execution tool, not a discovery tool — and that seems intentional.
