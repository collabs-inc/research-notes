# Lenny Rachitsky

Author of *Lenny's Newsletter* and host of *Lenny's Podcast* (ex-Airbnb PM); the central aggregator and amplifier of the product-and-AI discourse.

## Core Thesis

AI won't replace PMs; it transforms the role. Counterintuitively, he argues AI disrupts the *high-level strategic* work most, while the irreplaceable value becomes the "people stuff" — being the glue who aligns stakeholders and drives the business. From "How AI will impact product management" (2024):

> "Essentially, to be the conductor/quarterback/glue of the team who pulls all the disparate pieces together."

> "People stuff! Aligning opinionated stakeholders, unblocking blockers… getting buy-in on big ideas."
