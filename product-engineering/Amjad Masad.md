# Amjad Masad

Co-founder and CEO of [[Replit]]; the loudest voice for the "everyone is a software *creator*" reading of [[Everyone Is a Builder]].

## Core Thesis

Not a billion software *engineers* but a billion software *creators* — white-collar, non-technical people who describe what they want and let agents build it. *"The future of software is agents all the way down."* Replit positions itself around personal and agentic software creation (the autonomous Agent).

> A widely-circulated 2025 line — "we don't care about professional coders anymore" — appears in secondary coverage; treat as unverified until confirmed against the primary interview.

## Why It Matters

Masad represents the most radical democratization claim in the vault: pushing the "builder" frontier past the product team and past engineers to end users with no coding background. It's the consumer-facing extreme of the same force producing the [[The Product Engineer|product engineer]] — and it shares DNA with [[Geoffrey Litt]]'s [[Malleable Software|malleable software]] and [[Pete Koomen]]'s "agent builders."
