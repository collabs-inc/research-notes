# OpenAI

The AI lab (ChatGPT, Codex) whose product org is built around **technical PMs** who translate model capability into products — and whose leaders have set much of the vocabulary for this vault.

- **Org:** PMs "lead complex, technical products from idea to deployment, translating model capabilities into tools," working closely with research and engineering at the model layer. Specialized product teams (Codex, API Agents, Personalization, Integrity). Scale (secondary, approximate): ~4,500 employees, plans to roughly double by end of 2026.
- **Efficiency:** ~$6.5M revenue per employee (Epoch AI, May 2026) — see [[Revenue Per Employee]].

## Voices That Set the Frame

- **Sean Grove** — [[Specs Are the New Code]] ("The New Code," June 2025).
- **Kevin Weil (CPO):** *"Writing evals is going to become a core skill for product managers."* (see [[Evals as the New PRD]])
- **Sam Altman:** the [[The One-Person Company|one-person billion-dollar company]] betting pool, and taste as a hireable AI-era skill ([[Taste as the Bottleneck]]).

## Why It Matters

OpenAI built an early [[Forward-Deployed Engineer]] team (2025) and is a primary venue for the "specs/evals/taste" reframing of product work. Its "technical PM" model is the convergence from the *PM side* — PMs who are deeply technical.
