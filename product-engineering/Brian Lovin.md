# Brian Lovin

A designer-engineer now building AI products at [[Notion]]; the archetypal [[Design Engineer|design engineer]] operationalizing AI into the design loop.

- **Background:** Self-described "designer and software engineer." Co-founded **Campsite** (team-collaboration app, acquired by [[Notion]]); before that designed GitHub Mobile for ~4 years (joining GitHub via its acquisition of his startup Spectrum); earlier did payments design at Facebook/Instagram and was first product designer at Buffer. Co-hosted the *Design Details* podcast for ~9 years; created *Staff Design*, an interview project on the IC career path.

## 2025–26 AI/Craft Work

Lovin built a **"Prototype Playground"** — a shared coding environment for Notion's design team — and a custom **`/figma`** slash command that translates Figma designs into code via Claude Code, arguing that **code-based prototyping is the future for AI products.**

## Why It Matters

Lovin is the designer-founder fusing [[Craft|craft]] with agentic coding at a major product company — a live example of [[Design Engineer|design engineering]] meeting [[Vibe Coding|AI-assisted building]], and of [[Taste as the Bottleneck|taste]] applied through code at speed.
