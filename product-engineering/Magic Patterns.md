# Magic Patterns

An "AI prototype generator for product teams" — prompt → production-ready UI built against *your own design system.* Explicitly targets product teams, not engineers.

- **Funding:** $6M Series A (Nov 2025), led by Standard Capital (with Y Combinator and others).
- **Traction:** grew to $1M ARR with "zero full-time employees" before the round — itself a [[Revenue Per Employee|product-engineering-era artifact]] — with 1,500+ product teams as users.
- **Product:** Magic Patterns 2.0 added design-system import from Figma, Storybook, or any website via a Chrome extension — the design-system fidelity is the wedge (see [[Design Systems and Velocity]]).

## Why It Matters

Magic Patterns is squarely in the prototype-from-intent middle of [[The Product Engineering Stack]], and its "design-system-aware generation" is the bet that [[Craft as a Moat|brand fidelity]] is what product teams need from AI prototyping. The "$1M ARR, 0 FTEs" stat doubles as evidence for the [[Small Teams, Big Output|small-team]] thesis.
