# Marty Cagan

Founder/partner of Silicon Valley Product Group (SVPG); the "dean" of empowered product teams and author of *Inspired*, *Empowered*, and *Transformed* (2024).

## Core Thesis on AI

Cagan's 2026 position is that AI doesn't replace good product people but *exposes* weak ones. From "Product Coaching and AI" (Feb 2026), which he calls *"a significant change to what we have been advocating for the past two decades"*:

> "For many people, their use of AI is simply exposing the theater, rather than highlighting their potential contribution."

PMs who merely "aggregate requests, generate roadmaps, and create PRDs" are doing work "an agent — or your engineer or designer — could just as easily do themselves." The irreducible core is "thinking and judgment" on hard problems.

## Why It Matters

Cagan is the PM-world voice converging on the same conclusion as the builders and the engineers: as building gets cheap, *judgment about what to build* is what matters — the [[Taste as the Bottleneck]] thesis from the product-leadership side.
