# Alloy

"AI Prototyping for Product Managers." Addresses the "what to build" question from the validation angle.

- **Batch:** YC W23
- **Founders:** Simon Kubica (CEO, ex-Atlassian product leader on Jira/Forge), Christian Iacullo (CTO, ex-Canva)
- **Funding:** $3.5M seed (Feb 2025) from Blackbird and Bain Capital Ventures. Earlier $2M round.
- **Traction:** 42% of Forbes Cloud 100 and 17% of Fortune 500 signed up. Used at Harvey, HubSpot, Canva, SeatGeek, Linktree.
- **Team:** 5 people, Sydney, Australia
- **Status:** Active

## What They Build

Captures existing products via browser extension. PMs build interactive, brand-accurate prototypes through conversational AI. 30+ integrations (Linear, Notion, Jira Product Discovery). Originally focused on planning/prioritization, pivoted when they realized AI lets PMs "do the work rather than just plan it."

## Relevance to "Cursor for PMs"

Complementary to the full [[The Full Loop Gap|RFS vision]]. Alloy helps PMs rapidly build and test feature ideas before engineering commitment — the validation step between "we think we should build X" and "here are the specs for the coding agent."
