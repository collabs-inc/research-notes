# Cognee

Knowledge-graph-native memory infrastructure, focused on enterprise from day one.

## Company

- **Founded**: 2024 (Berlin)
- **Funding**: $7.5M seed (Feb 2026, Pebblebed + 42CAP). Angels from Google DeepMind, OpenAI, FAIR founders.
- **GitHub**: 17,545 stars, 1,851 forks (verified May 27 2026). [github.com/topoteretes/cognee](https://github.com/topoteretes/cognee)

## Architecture

ECL (Extract-Cognify-Load) pipeline that bootstraps durable knowledge from raw data:
- Ingests from 38+ data sources
- Produces [[Knowledge Graphs for Memory|knowledge graphs]] and semantic representations
- Graph-native storage and retrieval

## Traction

- Pipeline runs grew from ~2,000 to 1M+ (**500x**) in 2025
- Live in 70+ companies including Bayer (pharma research), University of Wyoming (policy documents), dltHub

## Strategic Position

Positioned as enterprise-grade memory infrastructure rather than developer-first middleware (contrast with [[Mem0]]'s community-first approach). Strong European presence (Berlin HQ) and GDPR awareness.
