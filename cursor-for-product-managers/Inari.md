# Inari

"Your junior AI product manager." The closest YC company to the full [[The Full Loop Gap|signal-to-spec]] vision. Acquired by Amplitude.

- **Batch:** YC S23
- **Founders:** Frank Lee (CEO, ex-Dapper Labs/Opendoor/Amazon), Eric Kim (CTO, ex-AWS SageMaker founding tech lead)
- **Funding:** $500K seed from YC
- **Team:** 2 people, San Francisco
- **Status:** Acquired by Amplitude (~June 2026). Founders joined Amplitude's AI Agents team.

## What They Built

Ingested feedback from Gong, Intercom, Zendesk, Slack. Auto-extracted actionable insights. Linked to CRM and backlog tools (Jira, Linear). Generated one-click PRDs.

## Significance

Previously the closest YC company to the full "signal-to-spec" vision described in [[YC RFS Spring 2026]]. The acquisition by Amplitude (alongside [[Kraftful]]) suggests the incumbent playbook: absorb the discovery startups rather than compete with them.
