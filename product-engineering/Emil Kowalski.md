# Emil Kowalski

A [[Design Engineer]] on [[Linear]]'s web team (previously on the design team at [[Vercel]]); known for widely-used open-source motion libraries and for teaching the craft.

## Notable Work

- **Sonner** (React toast) and **Vaul** (React drawer) — open-source libraries reported downloaded tens of millions of times per week combined (figure approximate).
- **"Animations on the Web"** — a paid course on web motion, a primary curriculum for aspiring design engineers.
- A **"Design Engineer" skill** packaging his craft heuristics for AI coding agents — an early-2025 example of *taste encoded for agents.*

## Why It Matters

Kowalski bridges craft-as-libraries and craft-as-education, and his agent "skill" is a leading signal of how [[Taste as the Bottleneck|taste]] gets operationalized for AI tools — a direct, practical instance of the [[Craft as a Moat|craft]] thesis meeting the agentic era. He's part of the [[Vercel]]→[[Linear]] [[Design Engineer|design-engineer]] diaspora.
