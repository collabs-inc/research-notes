# Tobi Lütke

Founder and CEO of [[Shopify]]; author of the era's defining [[The AI Headcount Mandate|AI-usage mandate]].

## The Memo (April 2025)

Lütke posted the internal memo publicly when it began leaking. The verified lines:

> "Reflexive AI usage is now a baseline expectation at Shopify."

> "Before asking for more headcount and resources, teams must demonstrate why they cannot get what they want done using AI."

> "Frankly, I don't think it's feasible to opt out of learning the skill of applying AI in your craft… I cannot see this working out today, and definitely not tomorrow."

> "Stagnation is slow-motion failure."

AI competency was folded into performance and peer reviews.

## Why It Matters

Lütke converted the [[Small Teams, Big Output|small-team]] thesis into corporate policy at scale — the clearest top-down forcing function pushing organizations toward fewer, more AI-fluent, more product-minded builders. Other CEOs ([[Amazon]]'s Jassy, Salesforce's Benioff) echoed the framing. See [[The AI Headcount Mandate]].
