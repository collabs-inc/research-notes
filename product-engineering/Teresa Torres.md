# Teresa Torres

Product-discovery coach and author of *Continuous Discovery Habits* (2021); originator of the [[Continuous Discovery]] methodology and the Opportunity Solution Tree.

## Core Contribution

[[Continuous Discovery]] — "at a minimum, weekly touchpoints with customers, by the team building the product, in pursuit of a desired outcome" — is one of the most widely adopted modern discovery frameworks. The Opportunity Solution Tree (2016) is its signature artifact.

## The AI Turn

Torres is a striking case of the [[PM–Engineer Convergence|convergence]] from the *discovery side*: with no engineering background, she began [[Vibe Coding|vibe coding]] in March 2025, reports writing more software in six months than in her lifetime, and shipped a production AI product (an interview-coaching tool). She finds vibe coding *"particularly powerful for assumption testing."* Her caution — *"just because you can doesn't mean you should"* — frames whether discovery people *should* build as a question of org setup, not tooling.

## Why It Matters

Torres shows the boundary dissolving in both directions: just as engineers gain product judgment, discovery experts gain the ability to build. She's the [[Prototyping as Thinking|prototyping-as-thinking]] thesis embodied.
