# Guillermo Rauch

Founder and CEO of [[Vercel]] (Next.js, [[v0]]); the most prominent champion of the [[Design Engineer]] role and the "everyone's a builder" thesis.

## Core Thesis

*"Everyone's an engineer now."* AI tools democratize product creation — Rauch's stated mission is to expand builders "from 5 million developers to over 100 million people" (see [[Everyone Is a Builder]]). Translation tasks (design → code) get automated; what remains crucial is conceptual understanding and "eloquence in describing what you want."

On Lenny's Podcast ("Everyone's an engineer now," April 2025), he names three AI-era skills: clearly defining product intent, coaching AI through iteration, and resolving problems when AI hits its limits. On [[Taste as the Bottleneck|taste]]: AI can *democratize* polish (v0 embeds best practices), and the way to build taste is "exposure hours" watching real users — feed the AI references and inspiration over prescriptive instructions, "because the AI might implement solutions better than you would have."

## Why It Matters

Rauch turned [[Design Engineer|design engineering]] into a recruited discipline at [[Vercel]] and is the clearest articulator of the optimistic, democratizing reading of [[The Product Engineer|product engineering]] — building as a broadly human activity, with [[Craft as a Moat|craft]] and curation as the differentiators.
