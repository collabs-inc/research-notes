# Market Landscape

Market sizing and structural overview for the [[YC RFS Spring 2026|"Cursor for Product Managers"]] category.

## Market Sizing

- **PM software market:** $12.5B (2024), 12.4% CAGR
- **Feedback/voting sub-segment:** $800M+
- No Gartner Magic Quadrant exists for this category
- Forrester is creating a new **"Customer Feedback Management and Analytics Solutions"** category (landscape report spring 2026) — first formal analyst recognition of the space

## Consolidation Wave (2024-2026)

## Implications

The acquisition pattern suggests two things:
1. There is validated demand for AI-powered product discovery
2. Standalone tools in this space struggle to reach escape velocity before incumbents absorb or outcompete them

A new entrant would need to either move faster than the acquisition cycle or build something architecturally different enough that bolting AI onto existing tools can't replicate it. See [[The Full Loop Gap]] for what that differentiation might look like.

## Skepticism Data Points

- 67% of PMs say generic AI PRDs need substantial rewriting
- MIT Project NANDA: 95% of enterprise generative AI pilots fail to deliver measurable ROI
- Stanford: 41% of YC AI startups were in "GPT wrapper" territory
- Series A shutdowns up 2.5x YoY in 2025
- Aakash Gupta tested 47 AI PM tools, kept 9 — "Most were garbage"
