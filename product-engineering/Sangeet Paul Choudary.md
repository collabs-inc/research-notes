# Sangeet Paul Choudary

Founder of Platformation Labs; author of *Platform Revolution* and *Reshuffle: Who Wins When AI Restacks the Knowledge Economy* (2025). The most structural voice on what AI does to *roles*.

## Core Thesis

AI doesn't just automate tasks — it *restacks* the knowledge economy. Agentic systems "disassemble" the tasks that were bundled into roles, optimize them across systems, and reallocate them — shifting who creates value and which players consolidate power.

## Why It Matters

Choudary gives the [[PM–Engineer Convergence|role-dissolution debate]] its theoretical frame: the merging of PM and engineer isn't a fad but an instance of a general pattern in which AI unbundles and rebundles work. Where [[Lenny Rachitsky]] sees continuity-with-transformation and [[Charity Majors]] sees a stubborn specialist core, Choudary describes the *mechanism* by which roles like the [[The Product Engineer|product engineer]] form: tasks come unbundled from the old titles and reassemble around whoever (or whatever) can do them best.
