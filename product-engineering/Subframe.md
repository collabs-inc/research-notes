# Subframe

"The AI design tool built for code" — a drag-and-drop visual editor plus component library that outputs clean React + Tailwind, aimed at people who want real design control rather than just code manipulation.

- Ships open-source CLI / Radix-based components. Funding history is thin publicly (early pre-seed; no confirmed later round found — treat as unverified beyond pre-seed).

## Why It Matters

Subframe represents the "design-system-as-source-of-truth → code" approach to the prototype→build handoff in [[The Product Engineering Stack]] — adjacent to [[Magic Patterns]] and [[Polymet]], distinguished by giving the builder fine-grained visual control. Part of the prototype-layer cohort tracked in [[Competitive Dynamics]].
