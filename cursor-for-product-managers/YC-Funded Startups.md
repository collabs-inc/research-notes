# YC-Funded Startups

Index of YC companies in or adjacent to the [[YC RFS Spring 2026|"Cursor for Product Managers"]] space.

## The Headline Finding

The three YC companies that came closest to the RFS vision are all dead or acquired. The Spring 2026 batch — the first after the RFS was published — did not produce a direct "Cursor for PMs" company.

## Pattern

The [[Competitive Dynamics|acquisition pattern]] is consistent: standalone AI PM tools get absorbed by incumbents (Amplitude took Kraftful + Inari, Reforge took Monterey AI) rather than scaling independently. The YC portfolio in this space skews toward the data collection layer (interviews, synthetic panels) rather than the synthesis-to-spec layer the RFS envisions.
