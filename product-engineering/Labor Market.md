# Labor Market

The demand-side evidence for [[The Product Engineer|product engineering]] — strong in direction, uneven in measurement.

## "Product Engineer" — Real but Measurement-Poor

- **Demand proxy:** [[PostHog]] cites an **89% rise in searches for "product engineers" since 2021** (search interest, not postings). Hacker News "Who's Hiring?" mentions roughly doubled (~2%→5%) by Feb 2024.
- **Compensation caveat:** Generic salary aggregators *conflate* the new software "product engineer" with the old manufacturing/hardware "product engineer," depressing the figures. There is **no clean levels.fyi "product engineer" track** — infer software-PE pay from senior full-stack/SWE bands (commonly $150K–$300K+ TC at startups/scaleups), don't trust the ~$77K–$101K aggregator numbers.
- **Postings (qualitative):** 2026 postings emphasize end-to-end ownership, data-analytics fluency, cross-functional translation, and an automation mindset; seniority is "scope and ambiguity," not years.

## The Cousins With Hard Numbers

The product engineer follows the same pattern as two better-documented titles:

- **[[AI Engineer]]:** #1 on LinkedIn's "Jobs on the Rise" 2025; ~75K US postings 2023–25; ~$245K average, premium rising with seniority.
- **[[Forward-Deployed Engineer]]:** postings +800% in 9 months of 2025 (candidate pool only +~50%); base $166K–$288K; ~83% in SF/NY; 59% at seed–Series A.

## Reference SWE Pay (levels.fyi, 2025)

Median US total comp: Entry $155K · Engineer $226K · Senior $312K · Staff $457K · Principal $551K (+3.5% median YoY). "AI/ML specialization has emerged as core engineering."

## Context: PM & Eng Demand (early 2026)

Lenny Rachitsky's tracker: 7,300+ open PM roles globally (highest in 3+ years); 67,000+ engineering roles; demand concentrated in SF/NY; "AI has not reduced engineering demand yet." See [[Will AI Replace Product Managers]].

## The Pattern

Demand clusters in SF + NY and skews early-stage and AI-native — so far the [[The Product Engineer|product-engineer-as-default-builder]] pattern is a *startup and scaleup* phenomenon led by [[Company Case Studies|product-led companies]].
