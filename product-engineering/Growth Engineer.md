# Growth Engineer

An engineer who ships to move metrics. Both growth engineers and [[The Product Engineer|product engineers]] build fast and talk to users, but the orientation differs: **"product engineers ship to build; growth engineers ship to learn."**

## The Distinction

- **Growth engineer:** runs A/B experiments, optimizes funnels (acquisition, activation, retention, monetization), works at high velocity with deliberately low test coverage. Owns *metric movement*.
- **Product engineer:** owns the *core product value* end-to-end, including quality and durability.

a16z signaled the role's rise by launching a **Growth Engineer Fellowship** (2026), framing it as evidence that "the roles of a growth marketer, PM, and engineer are all collapsing into one" — the same [[PM–Engineer Convergence|convergence]] thesis applied to the growth function.
