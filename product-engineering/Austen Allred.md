# Austen Allred

Founder of Gauntlet AI and co-founder/CEO of BloomTech (formerly Lambda School). Source of the cleanest one-line statement of [[PM–Engineer Convergence]].

## The Quote

> "I don't see how the roles of 'product manager' and 'engineer' don't get mashed together in the future. Totally reasonable for a person to figure out what to build and build it with AI."

— Austen Allred, X, Feb 2025. (Verified across multiple secondary reproductions; the original tweet itself was paywalled to direct fetch.)

## Gauntlet AI

Allred pivoted from BloomTech's junior-developer, student-funded model to **Gauntlet AI** — an intensive (reportedly ~100 hrs/week, ~12-week) program selecting for top-percentile ability and training "AI-first" engineers, funded by hiring partners rather than students. The bet: AI redefined what "developer" means, so training must produce a new kind of builder who thrives in an AI-first workflow.

## Why It Matters

Allred is the builder-side voice of convergence and a *forcing function* for it — explicitly retraining engineers for the [[Everyone Is a Builder]] world. His thesis pairs with [[The AI Headcount Mandate]] as the institutional accelerants of the shift.
