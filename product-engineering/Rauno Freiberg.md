# Rauno Freiberg

Staff Design Engineer at [[Vercel]] (previously Design Engineer at The Browser Company / Arc); the canonical "craft" [[Design Engineer|design engineer]]. His thesis: "creating software that makes people feel something."

## Notable Work

- **[[Web Interface Guidelines]]** — a checklist of interaction, animation, layout, form, and performance details that make a good web interface; adopted by [[Vercel]] (vercel.com/design/guidelines) and published openly.
- **Devouring Details** (2025) — an interactive interaction-design "manual," 23 chapters with 23 downloadable React components: *"everything I know about not just interactions, but design."*
- **rauno.me/craft** — a public log of micro-interaction experiments.

## Why It Matters

Freiberg is the reference figure for *detail-level* craft, and his artifacts exemplify how the [[Design Engineer|design-engineer]] diaspora spreads quality through *reusable references* rather than only through hiring — making [[Taste as the Bottleneck|taste]] explicit and shareable (and, increasingly, legible to AI agents). See [[Craft as a Moat]].
