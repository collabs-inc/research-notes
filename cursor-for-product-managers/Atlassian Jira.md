# Atlassian Jira

Most ambitious vision for [[The Full Loop Gap|the full loop]] among incumbents, but critical discovery pieces are still in early access.

## What's Shipped (GA)

- **Rovo AI** bundled into all paid Jira/Confluence/JSM plans (no separate charge). 5M+ MAU, 14M+ Rovo-assisted actions/month. Capabilities: search across Atlassian + third-party tools, conversational chat over organizational knowledge, agent assignment in Jira.
- **Rovo Studio** (no-code agent builder) — teams create multi-step agents with roles, approvals, versioning.
- **Jira Product Discovery** (Standard $10/user/mo, Premium $25/user/mo) — idea prioritization, roadmapping, published views. AI features today limited to text generation (brainstorming, summarization, cleanup). Free for up to 3 creators.
- **Rovo credits**: 25-150/user/month depending on tier.

## What's in Early Access / Coming

- **Feedback tool** — captures input from support tickets, sales calls, CRM, Slack, surveys. AI auto-organizes into actionable insights linked to JPD ideas. *The key missing piece.*
- **Product Collection** — "AI-powered product operating system" combining JPD + Feedback + Rovo + analytics integrations (starting with Pendo). *This is the full-loop vision.*
- **Agentic roadmapping** — AI suggests priorities, surfaces trade-offs. Still in "exploration phase."
- **Max Reasoning Mode** for Rovo — multi-step autonomous planning.

## Cycle Acquisition (Sept 2025)

Acquired Cycle, a French AI feedback platform, for ~$6M. Cycle's standalone product discontinued; being rebuilt inside JPD. This is the key bet — if Atlassian successfully embeds Cycle's AI feedback engine into JPD, they'll have signal-to-delivery in one platform. But execution risk is high.

## Trust Issues

Major controversy May 2026: Atlassian enabled default data collection for AI training with no visible opt-out toggle. HN reaction was overwhelmingly negative, with users planning migrations to Linear, Plane, YouTrack. Community forums also show complaints about Rovo hallucinating success on write operations. 30.8% faster PRs with Rovo Dev, but agents better at reading than writing.
